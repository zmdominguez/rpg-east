## 🕯️ Backstory

Temple: Last Grain (drought-prone farming village)
Role: Food distribution coordinator, harvest prayer leader

Years of Service: 3 years

Reputation: Effective but unorthodox, gets donations through charm and “persuasion”

## 👨‍🎤 Description

I’m a Half-Elf with a lean, agile build and a presence that tends to draw attention—sometimes the right kind, sometimes not. My hair falls just past my shoulders, usually tousled, either silver-blonde or jet black depending on who’s asking. One of my eyes shines with a golden glint, like something divine still lingers there; the other’s a stormy grey, always watching, always measuring. My skin’s a light olive shade, and when I channel my magic, it shimmers faintly—like the gods left fingerprints on me. I move like someone who knows where the exits are and how to make an impression before leaving through one.

## 📝 Notes

Secret: Still searching for his daughter; joined partly for access to church’s information network

Mantra: “The rat endures where the lion falls” 

Public Face: Devoted servant who understands hunger and serves those who suffer

Private Truth: Con artist using the church’s resources to find his child while genuinely trying to help others 

Church Skills He Uses: 
	∙ Persuasion (donor relations) 
	∙ Deception (forging donation receipts, “creative” accounting) 
	∙ Investigation (tracking missing persons through church records) 
	∙ Intimidation (when merchants need “convincing”)
