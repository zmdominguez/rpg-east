## 🕯️ Backstory

Snag Ironvow has one core memory that defines everything: at age seven, he found a rusty warhammer buried in the sand outside his village. He swung it, missed a rock, tripped, and landed face-first in the dirt — and at that exact moment, a dust storm cleared and sunlight broke through directly onto him.

He has never questioned what this meant. He has never needed to.

Snag spent the next fifteen years fighting and praying and fighting some more. He calls the object of his devotion the Holy One, and will not elaborate further — not because he is being coy, but because he genuinely has not thought about it. Is the Holy One a god? A force? An object? A person? Snag smiles when asked and says "the Holy One knows." That is his entire theology. He does not consider this a gap.

He joined Cross Bones Inc because they "needed a paladin," a fact the rest of Cross Bones disputes loudly and often. Jynx hired him for his shield and his complete inability to stay down in a fight. Nyra mostly finds him exhausting. Snag considers both of them his sacred charges.

He has never cast a spell. He is not troubled by this.


## 👨‍🎤 Description

Snag is a small goblin, even by goblin standards — wide bright yellow eyes, a gap-toothed grin that appears at deeply inappropriate moments, and a battered suit of chain mail two sizes too large that clanks with every step. He wears a battered tabard over the armor with a symbol drawn on it in what appears to be mud — it is unclear what the symbol depicts and Snag cannot explain it. His warhammer is dented. His shield has a fist-sized bite mark in it from something large. He carries a carved bone token on a cord around his neck that he calls a relic of the Holy One and keeps tucked against his chest, face-down.

He is the loudest person in any room he enters.
