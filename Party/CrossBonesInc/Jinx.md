## 🕯️ Backstory

Jinx doesn't talk much about where she's from — only that it was south, hot, and that she left before anyone could make her stay.

She grew up as an outcast. Fallen Aasimar born among desert traders don't get warm welcomes; the pale skin, the silver hair, the eyes that catch light wrong — people see something off about her and trust their instincts. She found work as a guide anyway. She knew the routes, the weather patterns, the places caravan masters paid well to avoid. She was good at it.

The fire came out the first time a raider put a blade to a merchant she liked. She didn't mean to ignite anything. It happened anyway.

After that, she stopped pretending the magic wasn't there. She stopped pretending a lot of things. Cross Bones found her three years later on a job in the Dustwall — they needed someone who could navigate and fight and not ask questions. She needed people who wouldn't flinch when her eyes went dark.

She still takes the guide jobs when Cross Bones is between contracts. Old habit. She likes knowing where the exits are.


## 👨‍🎤 Description

Jinx is angular and quiet — pale grey-silver skin, close-cropped white hair, eyes the colour of a desert sky just before a storm. She wears traveler's clothes over light armour and keeps a dagger strapped flat against her forearm. Nothing about her appearance announces danger until her eyes flicker with violet light, and then it's usually too late to reconsider.

When the Necrotic Shroud activates — which she reserves for situations that have already gone badly wrong — dark-feathered wings tear through her coat and her eyes go fully black. She looks like a shadow that learned to burn.

She speaks precisely and rarely.
