# The Web of Velthara - Main Campaign Lore

## The Big Bad: Velthara, The Crimson Weaver

**True Name:** Velthara
**Title:** The Crimson Weaver
**Species:** Ancient Phase Spider Queen

### Origin (What Velthara Believes)
Velthara arrived in Thornhaven through a portal after the campaign of the Thelasian Lost City. She discovered the bloodroot growing in the surrounding hills and found it could amplify her power. She has chosen Thornhaven as her new lair.

### True Origin (DM Only — see [[age-of-roots]] for full lore)

Velthara is not a naturally occurring creature. She was **created by Maerath**, the god of fate, memory, and portals, approximately 600 years ago. Maerath wove her from threads of divine will — not a transformed animal, not a mortal altered, but something genuinely new — and gave her a specific purpose: consume Blood Root and with it, the divine essence of the god **Gorethis the Verdant Tyrant**, who had dispersed himself into Blood Root plants as part of a scheme to accumulate enough divine power to surpass all other gods.

Velthara did her job. Over 110 years she consumed the Blood Root garden, pulling Gorethis's essence from the plants. He is now largely dormant — **most of his divine fragments are inside Velthara**, where they have been for six centuries.

Maerath intended to restore Velthara's memories once the crisis passed. He fell into divine sleep before he could. **Velthara has never known what she is, why she exists, or where her hunger comes from.**

She is not evil. She is a divine weapon with no off switch, consuming the one thing she was built to consume, growing powerful and strange and old without understanding any of it.

**The critical danger:** If Velthara is killed without first scattering Gorethis's fragments inside her, they will converge at the moment of her death. Gorethis wakes — not gradually but all at once, in his full accumulated power. This is the trap. The players cannot simply fight their way to this ending.

**The solution exists:** Aeldris Vorn, a Thelasian archmage who helped seal Gorethis, wrote the dispersal method in his tower in the Veiled Peaks. The fragments can be safely scattered into the earth before Velthara is stopped. See [[plot-twists]] for the reveal guide.

### Appearance
A massive spider the size of a small house, with legs spanning 40 feet. Her carapace is deep crimson-black, pulsing with veins that glow faintly red. Eight eyes gleam with ancient intelligence.

### Methods
Velthara's influence extends beyond physical attacks. She lures and manipulates humanoids to build her power base in the city. She has infiltrated the **Church of Scarsith** and uses them to move against [[Eman]].

## The Bloodroot Connection

### What the Villagers Know
Bloodroot is a valuable medicinal herb that treats infections and numbs pain.

### The Truth
Velthara discovered the bloodroot can be processed to enhance her abilities and extend her influence over others.

### Naven's Role
[[Naven]] is the town herbalist. She maintains the bloodroot supply for legitimate medicinal purposes and is unaware of Velthara's interest in the plant.

## Velthara's Goals

1. Establish Thornhaven as her permanent lair
2. Use the Church of Scarsith to undermine Eman's authority
3. Build a network of influenced humanoids in the city
4. Harvest bloodroot to grow her power
