# Factions of Thornhaven

The major powers shaping the city and its future.

---

## Cross Bones Inc

**Type:** Growing mercantile and adventuring guild
**Base:** [[Cross Bones Pub]]
**Leader:** [[Mills]]

### What They Are
What started as a simple tavern has evolved into an institution. Cross Bones Inc operates as a guild that funds expeditions, trades in recovered artifacts, and provides protection services. They are becoming a economic force in the region.

### Agenda
- **Expand influence** through trade routes to [[Aussen]] and beyond
- **Profit from exploration** - funding adventurers in exchange for shares of discoveries
- **Gain political weight** - Mills wants a seat at the table when decisions are made
- **Protect investments** - problems are good for business

### Current Operations
- Hired the party to investigate disturbances
- Trading recovered goods from expeditions
- Building relationships with [[Grewdil Pierce]] in Aussen

---

## The Church of Scarsith, The Gnawing Hunger

**Type:** Religious institution
**Base:** [[Scarsith Temple]]
**Leader:** [[Nell]] (High Priestess)

### The God
**Scarsith** is the god of survival, scarcity, and perseverance. Depicted as a great rat with eyes that see through darkness, Scarsith teaches that the meek inherit what the strong abandon. The hungry endure while the fed grow weak.

### What They Are
A legitimate faith that grew during the hard years of the settlement. The poor, the forgotten, and those who survived famine found comfort in Scarsith's teachings. The church feeds the hungry and shelters the desperate.

### Agenda
- **Challenge Eman's authority** - Nell believes the mayor has forgotten the common people
- **Expand the faithful** - more followers means more power
- **Control food distribution** - whoever feeds the people, leads the people
- **Undermine the elite** - the rich built their fortune on the backs of believers

### Hidden Truth
Velthara has infiltrated some of the priests. Nell herself may or may not be aware - but the church's anti-Eman stance serves the spider's goals perfectly.

---

## The Village Government

**Type:** Political authority
**Base:** Town Hall
**Leader:** [[Eman]] (Mayor)

### What They Are
The official government of Thornhaven. Eman rules with the support of the army under [[Reven]] and maintains order through law and force when needed.

### Agenda
- **Maintain control** - Eman worked hard to get here and won't give it up
- **Keep the peace** - stability means her position is secure
- **Suppress the Owl Cult** - her former allies are now her greatest shame
- **Manage the factions** - balance Cross Bones' ambition and the Church's populism

### Secret
Eman was sent by the [[Owl Cult]] as a child to sabotage the settlement. She chose power instead. She fears her past being exposed more than anything.

---

## The Owl Cult

**Type:** Druidic circle
**Base:** [[Halfheight Forest]]
**Leader:** [[Gravy]] (Eldest Owl)

### What They Are
Ancient druids cursed to take owl form. They see the forest as sacred and the settlement as an invasion. The Saving War pushed them back, but they haven't forgotten.

### Agenda
- **Reclaim Eman** - she belongs to them, not the city
- **Protect the forest** - expansion must stop
- **Wait and watch** - patience is their weapon
- **Expose the traitor** - if Eman won't return, her secret will destroy her

### Current Status
Weakened after the Saving War but not defeated. They observe from the trees.

---

## Velthara's Web

**Type:** Hidden threat
**Base:** Unknown location near Thornhaven
**Leader:** [[Velthara|Velthara, the Crimson Weaver]]

*See: [[the-web-of-velthara]]*

### Agenda
- Use the Church of Scarsith against Eman
- Harvest bloodroot for power
- Make Thornhaven her lair
- Build a network of influenced servants
