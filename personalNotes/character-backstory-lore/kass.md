## The Falcon's Letter — Past Catching Up

### What Kass Knows

His falcon can deliver mail to major buildings in [[Thornhaven]] — the [[TownHall]], the [[Scarsith Temple]], the [[Library]], the cemetery. The bird goes where he directs it and has always come back.

**After S07 — added when the falcon returns with the letter:**
- The falcon returned from a task (or went out on its own — it does that now, blood root quirk) carrying a small sealed note.
- The wax seal is one Kass recognises. It is not from Thornhaven.
- The contents of the note are DM-determined — see below.

### DM Truth

**Kass's backstory is not yet established.** This thread is a placeholder with structure — fill in the specifics when his backstory is written with the player.

**The note — DM picks one when ready:**
- A warning: *"Don't trust the one who wears green in the market."*
- A demand: *"Meet me at the old gate at midnight. Come alone."*
- A name and nothing else — someone Kass hoped not to see in Thornhaven again.

**What this establishes:**
Someone from Kass's past is in Thornhaven or knows he is here. The falcon being the delivery method is not coincidental — whoever sent it knows how the bird works, which means they know Kass.

**When his backstory is written, connect:**
- Who sent the note and why
- What the seal represents (a family? an organisation? a place?)
- Whether the sender is in the city or communicating from outside it
- What they want from Kass and whether it's a threat, a warning, or a plea

## Double Blood Root Exposure

### What Kass Knows

He stole blood root in [[Rootmother's Garden]] during S05 — a small amount, kept. Then he participated in the full attunement ritual in S06. He has had it twice. He felt the difference.

Faint root-pattern veins appear under his skin when he grips something tightly. They fade quickly. He has not told the party how often it happens.

### DM Truth

See [[09-blood-root-effects]] — Kass's section. He is progressing faster than the others through the blood root stages.

**The falcon's behaviour:**
The bird is reacting to the blood root in Kass's blood. It refuses to deliver messages to locations it "dislikes" — returning early without explanation. It stays physically closer to him than it ever did before. It is not protecting him. It is responding to the root network signal in him, the same way plants in the room lean toward him. The falcon is, without understanding it, treating him like a root-oak.

**The key implication:**
Kass may reach Stage 2 (Speaking) before the party understands what Stage 1 looks like. The root network will begin communicating with him before anyone is ready for it. Unlike Selune — who was built for this — Kass was not. The message fragments he receives will not make sense to him. They may feel like intrusive thoughts, or like remembering something he was never told.

This is the most unpredictable of the four blood root threads. Handle carefully with Marco — give him the mechanical moments (roots cracking through wood, the falcon's strangeness) before introducing the cognitive ones.
