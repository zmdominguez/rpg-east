## The Silver Eclipse — Origins

### What Selune Knows

She has no memory of being left. Only fragments that surface in dreams: cold hands, a sound like chanting in a language she doesn't recognise, and a symbol carved into stone — a crescent eclipsing a circle. The silver eclipse.

She knows the druids of the Circle of the Moon found her at the roots of a white oak. She knows they were uneasy about her. She has always believed something shaped her for a dark purpose — and has spent her life refusing to become it.

**After S07 — added when she speaks with [[Brother Toben]]:**
- The silver eclipse is real and documented. It is the sigil of a druidic circle older than the [[Owl Cult]].
- A woman died in the [[Scarsith Temple]] approximately thirty years ago, speaking of a child left unfinished at a root-oak.
- There is a carved stone near the edge of [[Halfheight Forest]]. It is the only surviving root-oak site on a list found in the temple's lower archive.
- Toben has a sketch of the sigil, a partial ritual text, and a map fragment. She has read them.

### DM Truth

**What the eclipse circle was:**
A druidic circle that predates both the [[Owl Cult]] and the Thelasian civilisation. They believed the lunar eclipse was a threshold — a moment when something could pass between the mortal world and the deep root network. They performed their most significant rituals at sites where ancient root-oaks grew over root-network junctions. They left children at those sites as living vessels, encoded during the eclipse.

**What Selune actually is:**
A message vessel. The root network has been trying to communicate something to the mortal world for decades and cannot speak directly. The circle encoded the message in Selune during the eclipse — it lives in her, waiting to be delivered to the right place. It is not corruption. It is not ruin. It is a warning.

The message concerns [[Velthara]]. The root network detected Velthara's presence and her intentions toward the network itself long before any mortal did. Selune is the warning that never reached anyone.

**Why the ritual was incomplete:**
Someone interrupted it. The circle scattered. The woman who died in Toben's temple was an [[Owl Cult]] observer sent to watch — not participate — and she was injured in the interruption. [[Gravy]] was also there. He saw what happened. He has never spoken of it.

Who interrupted it: DM to decide. Candidates — an agent of [[Velthara]] who sensed what the circle was doing, or someone within the Owl Cult who feared what the child would become.

**The blood root acceleration:**
The attunement ritual in [[Rootmother's Garden]] is connecting Selune more deeply to the root network. At Stage 2 (Speaking), she will begin receiving fragments that feel like someone else's memories — they are not. They are the encoded message beginning to surface. They will feel like Velthara's movements seen from underground.

**The stone in Halfheight Forest:**
When Selune touches the carved stone, the encoding completes. The message surfaces — not as words, but as a map: the layout of Velthara's web and specifically where it connects to and is weakest against the root network. This is the weapon the roots have been holding for decades.

**Gravy's role:**
When Selune mentions the silver eclipse to [[Gravy]] in [[Halfheight Forest]], he goes still. He was there. Choosing the Owl Cult path and Selune's origin quest converge at the same stone, the same forest, the same conversation.

He will eventually say: *"The ritual was interrupted. Someone came who shouldn't have been there. The circle scattered. I thought the child was lost."*

He will not immediately say who interrupted it. That is a later thread.

## Blood Root — Selune's Exposure

### What Selune Knows

She performed the attunement ritual willingly in [[Rootmother's Garden]]. She knew what blood root was — she recalled the ancient druid ritual herself and led it. She does not know the long-term effects.

She has begun to sense large root systems within 30 feet — an instinct, like knowing where water is. She does not understand what this is yet.

### DM Truth

See [[09-blood-root-effects]] — Selune's section. Her connection to the root network is the deepest of the four, and it is accelerating the message-vessel encoding. The blood root attunement and her origin are not separate threads. They are the same thread.

She is becoming more connected to the network at exactly the moment the network is trying to speak through her. This is not coincidence.
