# Session S07: Return to Thornhaven

**Party Level:** 5
**Type:** Political / Social — no planned combat
**Location:** [[Thornhaven]]

## Context

The party arrives at the gates of [[Thornhaven]] at dawn after leaving [[Rootmother's Garden]]. Each member carries a blood-root-attuned item. [[Selune]] holds [[Pipe Ashford]]'s letter addressed to [[Roderick Ashford]]. [[Kass]] has double blood root exposure.

**Open homework from S06:**
- [ ] Deliver Pipe's letter to [[Roderick Ashford]]
- [ ] Return to [[Thornhaven]] and face [[Eman]]

---

## The Day — Scene by Scene

### Morning — Arrival & Eman

The party enters the city. Within an hour, a city guard in dress uniform finds them — [[Eman]] has eyes on the gate.

> *"The Mayor extends her compliments and requests the pleasure of your company at [[TownHall]], at your earliest convenience."*

**Full scene:** [[01-arrival-and-state]] → [[02-eman-encounter]]

**Eman's play:** She admits hiring [[Cross Bones Inc]], frames it as concern for [[Roderick Ashford]], and asks for silence. She owes the party **100gp each + a favour** from the S04 contract — but she won't offer it unless pressed. See negotiation ladder in [[02-eman-encounter]].

---

### Late Morning — Roderick & Payment

The party delivers [[Pipe Ashford]]'s letter to [[Roderick Ashford]] at his house on the eastern edge of the city. He pays the S04 contract (**40gp**) without being asked. He gives them **Maren's Weeping Candle** — a Zone of Truth item with a crying curse, handmade by his late wife.

**Full scene:** [[03-roderick-ashford]]

---

### Late Morning — The Market Stall (Mirr)

Passing through the [[Thornhaven]] morning market, [[Mirr]] spots a stall selling fresh Thelasian goods — including a piece with a maker's mark from a supposedly sealed workshop. The stall owner bought them from a frightened man coming from the direction of [[Aussen]].

If investigated at the [[Library]], [[Anni]] can confirm the workshop origins (History DC 14) — and mentions someone else recently requested records on the same region. The request was unsigned. *([[Velthara]]'s breadcrumb.)*

**Full scene:** [[04-market-stall]]

---

### Midday — Nell's Invitation Arrives

Wherever the party is, a Scarsith acolyte delivers a sealed note from [[Nell]].

> *"The High Priestess of Scarsith invites you to share a meal at the Temple. Scarsith provides for those who help others find what they need."*

No answer expected. The invitation is open until dusk.

---

### Afternoon — The Scarsith Temple (if they go)

[[Nell]] receives the party at the [[Scarsith Temple]] over a modest meal. After a long, careful setup about the city's poor, she asks: carry a census message to the eastern farms, discretely, because [[Eman]]'s office is watching her couriers.

[[Brother Toben]] — an old, quiet temple priest — watches [[Selune]] throughout the meal. Afterward he approaches her alone with one question: *"The silver eclipse. Where did you see it?"*

**Full scene:** [[05-temple-nell]]

---

### Late Afternoon — Mills at the Cross Bones Pub

[[Mills]] is waiting at her corner table at the [[Cross Bones Pub]] with two drinks poured. She acknowledges the Cross Bones mission without bitterness and makes her pitch: go to the Thelasian ruins near [[Aussen]], assess what [[Dr Clair Bucklebub]] is actually doing there, recover what's being let out of the dig.

Payment: **100gp per party member** (125gp if the party raises the market stall). Deadline: dawn tomorrow.

> *"Sleep on it. Find me here at dawn."*

**Full scene:** [[06-mills-crossbones]]

---

### Evening — The Familiar Face (Aeris)

During her regular set at the [[Cross Bones Pub]], [[Aeris Whisperwind]] sees a face in the crowd she recognizes from Elarion's court — there the night Thalen performed his last show. The figure leaves when she looks. Outside: gone.

*Thread: [[Sera Voss]]. Details in [[10-character-hooks]].*

---

### After Dark — The Owl (Selune)

An owl lands on [[Selune]]'s windowsill at night. It drops a white feather and leaves.

At next dusk, the owl returns and waits. If followed, it leads to the edge of [[Halfheight Forest]] — where [[Gravy]] waits and shifts to humanoid.

> *"You know the old ritual. You know what you started. Do you know what you'll become?"*

He offers knowledge of what the blood root transformation does, and methods to slow it — if the party comes into the forest under his terms. One chance only: if [[Selune]] doesn't follow at next dusk, he doesn't send another feather.

**Full scene:** [[07-owl-gravy]]

---

### Dawn — The Decision

[[Mills]] is at the [[Cross Bones Pub]] at dawn, expecting an answer. The owl feather expires at next dusk. [[Nell]]'s census mission waits at the [[Scarsith Temple]].

The party can only choose one path. Thornhaven is small — everyone sees who rides out with whom, and faction trust is earned by being chosen, not hedged.

**Full scene:** [[08-morning-decision]]
