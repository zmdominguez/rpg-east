# Cross Bones Pub — Mills's Pitch

**Time:** Late afternoon
**Location:** [[Cross Bones Pub]]
**Key NPCs:** [[Mills]], [[Aeris Whisperwind]] (evening)

## Mills Finds Them

[[Mills]] doesn't send a note. She waits at the [[Cross Bones Pub]] — at a corner table, two drinks already poured. If the party doesn't come to the pub, she sends a short message: *"Corner table. When you're ready."*

She buys a round when they arrive.

> *"You stopped something I was paid to finish. I don't love it — but I respect it. Cross Bones doesn't make enemies over professional disagreements."*

## The Pitch

> *"I have a job. The Thelasian ruins near [[Aussen]] — a scholar named [[Dr Clair Bucklebub]] is running an excavation there. Things have been coming out of that dig that shouldn't be turning up in the morning market. I want someone I trust to go in, assess what's actually happening, and recover anything significant before Clair locks it down behind academic paperwork."*

**If the party mentions Mirr's market stall:** Mills nods slowly.

> *"So it's already reaching the city. That's worse than I thought."*

She adds 25gp per party member to the offer on the spot, without being asked.

## What She Doesn't Say

- She doesn't know [[Dr Clair Bucklebub]] is compromised. She senses something is wrong with the dig and she can't put her finger on it.
- A previous Cross Bones contact she sent to look around the ruins came back evasive. Not scared — evasive, which bothered her more.
- She wants people she can trust precisely because she can't send her own crew without showing her hand.

## The Deadline

> *"Sleep on it. Find me here at dawn. If you're not here, I'll assume you're busy."*

She means it. She'll find someone else and not hold it against them.

## Payment

Base offer: **100gp per party member** on return with an accurate report of what's happening at the dig. Recovery of significant artifacts adds to this.

With the market stall mentioned: **125gp per party member**.

## The Path This Opens

Leave [[Thornhaven]], travel to [[Aussen]], enter the Thelasian excavation site, determine what [[Dr Clair Bucklebub]] is actually doing and why goods are leaving the site. Active danger likely. Artifacts likely. [[Velthara]]'s shadow over the whole thing — though the party doesn't know that yet.

## Evening — Aeris's Familiar Face

Later the same evening, [[Aeris Whisperwind]] plays her regular set at the [[Cross Bones Pub]].

Midway through a song, she sees a face across the room. She knows this person — not the name, not the full memory — but the face belongs to Elarion's court. It was there the night Thalen performed his last private show.

The figure is watching her perform. When Aeris meets their gaze, they don't look away immediately — and then they leave.

**If Aeris follows:** The crowd swallows the figure before she clears the room. Outside the pub: gone. The street is quiet.

*Don't resolve this. It's a thread. See [[10-character-hooks]] — Aeris's thread. The figure is [[Sera Voss]].*
