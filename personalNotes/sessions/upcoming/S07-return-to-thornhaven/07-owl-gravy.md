# Halfheight Forest Edge — Gravy's Warning

**Time:** After dark (night 1) — the feather. Next dusk — the meeting.
**Location:** [[Halfheight Forest]] edge (north gate, ten-minute walk)
**Key NPCs:** [[Gravy]], [[Selune]]

## Night One — The Feather

On the first night back in Thornhaven, an owl lands on the windowsill of wherever [[Selune]] sleeps. It looks at her for a moment. It drops a single white feather. It leaves without a sound.

No note. No voice.

Run this as a brief, quiet moment. Let Selune sit with it.

## Next Dusk — The Meeting

At dusk the following day, the owl returns to the same windowsill and waits. If Selune follows, it leads her north — through the city, out the north gate, ten minutes along the road — to the edge of [[Halfheight Forest]].

[[Gravy]] is perched on a low branch in owl form. When Selune approaches, he shifts to humanoid — unhurried, like someone who has been sitting in trees for a very long time.

> *"You know the old ritual. You know what you started. Do you know what you'll become?"*

He doesn't explain further unless pressed. He lets the question sit.

## What He Knows

- The blood root attunement performed in [[Rootmother's Garden]] has connected all four party members to the root network. It is not reversible on its own.
- The process has stages. He has seen it before — in druids who overexposed themselves. He knows what comes next.
- [[Kass]]'s double exposure concerns him specifically, though he won't say how he knows about it unless asked. (*The forest told him.*)
- He has methods to slow the progression. Not stop it — slow it.

## His Offer

He doesn't frame it as an offer. He frames it as an observation:

> *"I won't come to the city again. If you want what I know — and the chance to understand what you agreed to in that garden — you know where the trees begin."*

**If Selune asks what it costs:**
> *"Time. Patience. And coming in without weapons drawn."*

**If Selune asks about her own origins:**

He goes quiet for a moment. His eyes — still owl-yellow even in humanoid form — study her.

> *"The forest remembers older things than I do. Come in and ask it yourself."*

He shifts back to owl form and is gone.

## What He's Really Doing

Eman came from this cult as a child, and she chose the city over the forest. [[Gravy]] has been watching her ever since. He wants to know if these outsiders can be trusted — as instruments to pull her back, or at minimum to expose her.

He won't say any of this. Not yet.

## The Deadline

This is the only time the owl comes. If Selune doesn't follow at next dusk, [[Gravy]] does not send another feather. The offer is gone.

## The Path This Opens

Enter [[Halfheight Forest]] under Gravy's terms — no set timeline, no clear endpoint. Learn what the blood root transformation does and what Selune's origin connects to. Personal. Slow. The Owl Cult's political agenda surfaces only once trust is built.

**If the whole party comes:** Gravy accepts it. He won't turn anyone away. But his interest is specifically in [[Selune]], and the others will feel like guests in her conversation.
