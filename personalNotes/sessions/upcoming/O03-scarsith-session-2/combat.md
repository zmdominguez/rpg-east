# Combat Encounters

## Spiderling Swarm

**Location:** Level 2 - The Crypts
**Trigger:** Party is loud or destructive
**Difficulty:** Easy

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Spiderling Swarm | 1 | 36 | 12 | +5 | 2d6 piercing | Phase Step, poison, swarm immunities |

**Stats:**

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 3 (-4) | 13 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 1 (-5) | 7 (-2) | 1 (-5) |

- Speed: 30 ft, climb 30 ft
- Resistances: Bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
- Immunities: Charmed, frightened, paralyzed, prone

**Abilities:**
- **Bite:** DC 12 CON or poisoned 1 minute. Damage drops to 1d6 below half HP.
- **Phase Step (Recharge 5-6):** Teleport up to 15 ft.

**Weakness:** Fire and radiant deal +50% damage. Bright holy light (Sacred Flame, Daylight) forces DC 12 WIS or flee.

**Behavior:** Swarms isolated targets, avoids bright light, retreats toward Level 3 below 10 HP.

**What Party Sees:** Golden motes of light swarming together (actually tiny spiders).

## Web Guardian

**Location:** Level 3 entrance
**Trigger:** Party approaches Level 3
**Difficulty:** Medium

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Web Guardian | 1 | 68 | 14 | +6 | 1d8+3 slashing | Multiattack, Web Spray, Regeneration |

Large spider construct of red threads.

**Stats:**

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 3 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 4 (-3) |

- Speed: 40 ft, climb 40 ft
- Immunities: Poison, psychic

**Abilities:**
- **Multiattack:** Two leg slash attacks (reach 10 ft).
- **Web Spray (Recharge 5-6):** 20 ft cone, DC 13 DEX or restrained. Escape DC 13 STR. Web AC 10, 15 HP, vulnerable to fire.
- **Regeneration:** 5 HP/turn if within 10 ft of bloodroot. Stops if hit by fire/radiant.

**Weakness:** Destroy bloodroot patch (AC 8, 20 HP, vulnerable to fire) - stops regen, deals 10 damage.

**Behavior:** Guards Level 3, won't pursue beyond chamber, prioritizes restrained targets, fights to destruction.

**What Party Sees:** A golden construct of woven light (actually red spider threads).

## Partially Corrupted Bramwell

**Use if:** Party confronts Bramwell after surviving the trap.
**Difficulty:** Medium

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Partially Corrupted Bramwell | 1 | 38 | 14 | +2 | 1d6 blud + 1d4 poison | Spellcaster, Web Spit, Halfling Luck |

**Stats:**

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 9 (-1) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 11 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) |

- Speed: 25 ft, climb 15 ft
- Spellcasting: 3rd-level Cleric (DC 12, +4 spell attack)

**Abilities:**
- **Web Spit (Recharge 5-6):** 15 ft cone, DC 12 DEX or restrained. Escape DC 12 STR.
- **Whisper of the Weaver (Bonus):** 30 ft, DC 12 WIS or frightened 1 turn.
- **Halfling Luck:** Reroll natural 1s.

**Spells:**
| Level | Spells |
|-------|--------|
| Cantrips | Toll the Dead, Spare the Dying |
| 1st | Inflict Wounds (3d10), Sanctuary |
| 2nd | Hold Person, Spiritual Weapon (spider legs, 1d8+2) |

**Weakness:** Scarsith holy symbols deal +1d6 radiant. Radiant damage forces DC 11 WIS or lose concentration.

**Tactics:** Opens Hold Person, maintains Spiritual Weapon, closes for Inflict Wounds. Speaks in two voices - his own pleading, the Weaver's commanding. Does NOT flee.

**What Party Sees (If Honey Pot Still Active):** Bramwell surrounded by golden light, fighting defensively. If honey pot broken, they see spider legs emerging from his back, red threads in his eyes.

**Saving Bramwell:** At 0 HP can be stabilized. Lesser Restoration removes partial corruption. Without magical healing within 1 hour, corruption spreads (use Fully Corrupted stats below).

## Fully Corrupted Bramwell

**Use if:** Bramwell has time to fully transform, or party fails to save him.
**Difficulty:** Hard

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Corrupted Bramwell | 1 | 52 | 15 | +4 | 1d6+1 blud + 1d6 poison | Spellcaster, Web Spit, Whisper |

**Stats:**

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 16 (+3) | 10 (+0) |

- Speed: 30 ft, climb 20 ft
- Spellcasting: 4th-level Cleric (DC 13, +5 spell attack)

**Abilities:**
- **Web Spit (Recharge 5-6):** 15 ft cone, DC 13 DEX or restrained.
- **Whisper of the Weaver (Bonus):** 30 ft, DC 13 WIS or frightened 1 turn.

**Spells:**
| Level | Spells |
|-------|--------|
| Cantrips | Toll the Dead, Thaumaturgy |
| 1st | Inflict Wounds (3d10), Shield of Faith |
| 2nd | Hold Person, Spiritual Weapon (spider legs, 1d8+3) |

**Weakness:** Scarsith holy symbols deal +1d6 radiant. Radiant damage causes DC 12 WIS or stunned 1 turn.

**Tactics:** Same as partial, but more aggressive. Below 15 HP may briefly regain control ("Help me... please...") before corruption reasserts.

**Saving Him:** Greater Restoration or 1-hour purification ritual (DC 15 Religion, 100gp materials).
