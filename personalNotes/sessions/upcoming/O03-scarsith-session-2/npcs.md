# NPCs

## Brother Bramwell (Halfling)

**Role:** Secret antagonist (party thinks he's an ally)
**Truth:** First priest corrupted by [[Velthara]]

### Appearance
Older halfling with gray-streaked curly hair, laugh lines around his mouth. Bare feet. Priestly robes.

### How He Acts (To Deceive the Party)
- Warm, patient, fatherly
- Speaks softly, uses inclusive language ("we," "together")
- Occasionally pauses mid-sentence, staring at nothing (the corruption)
- Mentions being "hungry" in strange ways

### What He Wants
- Convert the party to Velthara's service
- Eliminate Sister Wren (she suspects him)
- Frame Nell as the corrupt one
- Expand Velthara's influence in the temple

### Manipulation Tactics
1. **Uses the cellar as "proof"** - Shows them spider corruption that looks like evidence of Nell's hoarding and political deals
2. **Prevents destruction** - Stops them from destroying the shrine ("we need evidence first!")
3. **Isolates them from Nell** - Keeps them busy, warns them not to talk to her
4. **Creates urgency** - "We must act soon, before she consolidates more power"
5. **Flatters them** - "You're the only ones I can trust with this"

### If They Try to Destroy the Shrine
He will:
- Physically block them: "Wait! We need witnesses first!"
- Appeal to strategy: "Destroy this and she'll claim we're lying"
- Show subtle anger (DC 14 Insight to notice he's upset)
- If they insist, he may attack (use Partially Corrupted stats)

### Key Lines

**About the honey:**
> "The honey helps you see clearly. Cuts through the illusions Nell has woven."

**About Nell's "devotees":**
> "She invites faithful new people to the city under emergency excuses. Simple folk who won't challenge her. You're different - you can see the truth."

**In the cellar:**
> "Do you see now? This is where The Sharing donations really go."

> "Eman's seal on those documents. They've been working together for years."

> "That belt? A relic of great power. She keeps it locked away, claiming it's 'too dangerous.' She fears someone becoming strong enough to challenge her."

**If they want to destroy things:**
> "Wait! We need this as evidence. Destroy it now and she'll claim we're lying."

**Building trust:**
> "I've served Scarsith faithfully for forty years. I know corruption when I see it. Trust me."

## Sister Nell

**Role:** Actually the good guy (party thinks she's suspicious)
**Truth:** Pure-hearted senior priestess, not yet corrupted

### Appearance
Human woman in her 30s, sharp eyes, practical robes. Projects authority.

### How She Acts (To Create Suspicion)
- Cold and distant to the party (she doesn't trust them after they failed her test)
- Busy, dismissive, always hurrying somewhere
- Speaks curtly, doesn't explain herself
- Occasionally watches them with narrowed eyes

### What She Wants
- Protect the church from corruption
- Expand The Sharing to feed more poor
- Counter Eman's political influence
- Figure out why these visiting clerics seem off

### Key Lines
> "I have matters to attend to. Brother Bramwell will see to your needs."

> "The Sharing happens at dawn. You're welcome to observe."

(Keep interactions brief and cool to contrast with Bramwell's warmth)

## Sister Wren (Halfling)

**Role:** The party's only chance at redemption
**Truth:** Knows something is wrong with Bramwell, hasn't drunk the honey

### Appearance
Halfling woman in healer's robes. Red-rimmed eyes from crying. Nervous, constantly looking over her shoulder.

### How She Acts
- Scared, desperate
- Speaks quickly, afraid of being overheard
- Seems slightly paranoid (which makes party doubt her)
- Genuine grief for her old friend Bramwell

### What She Wants
- Save Bramwell if possible
- Expose whatever changed him
- Protect the temple from whatever is spreading

### Key Lines
> "He was my closest friend. Twenty years. But something changed him."

> "That honey he gives people... don't you feel strange? Like you can't quite see things right?"

> "Please. There's a place I need to show you. It's the only way you'll understand."

### The Trap
Wren knows about a hidden chamber where Bramwell has been meeting "something." What she doesn't know is that Bramwell set a trap there specifically for her. If the party follows her, they walk into the trap - but surviving it reveals the truth.
