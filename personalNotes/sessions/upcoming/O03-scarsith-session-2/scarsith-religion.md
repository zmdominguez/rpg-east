---

## The Sharing

Every seventh day, the temple distributes food to anyone.

- Happens at dawn in the temple courtyard
- Priests bless each portion: "What is taken must be shared"
- Recipients respond: "What is shared sustains us all"

**Party's cover story:** Visiting clergy here to help with The Sharing.

---

## Clergy Appearance

- Small rat skull pendant
- Robes with gnaw-mark patterns on hem
