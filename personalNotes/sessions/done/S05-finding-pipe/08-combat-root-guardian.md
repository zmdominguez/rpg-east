# Combat: The Rootmother's Echo (Optional)

**Trigger:** Party attempts to take Blood Root without permission, or tries to force [[Pipe Ashford]] to leave.

## Read Aloud

> The ground shudders. The shrine cracks. Roots explode from the earth — not the thin tendrils of a plant, but ancient, massive things, thick as tree trunks — coiling and lashing upward. The shape that rises is *enormous*, towering over the stone pillars, blotting out a piece of the sky. It has no face. It has no eyes. And it doesn't need them.

## Creature

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |

## Root Guardian Stats

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 21 (+5) | 6 (-2) | 20 (+5) | 3 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 3 (-4) |

- **Size:** Huge
- **Speed:** 20 ft
- **Reach:** 10 ft
- **Resistances:** Cold, fire
- **Immunity:** Lightning (absorbed as healing)
- **Condition Immunity:** Blinded, deafened, exhaustion, prone

**Multiattack:** One Hammer strike and one Rock throw.

**Hammer:** +8 to hit, reach 10 ft, 2d12+5 bludgeoning (~18 avg). On hit, target DC 15 STR save or knocked prone and pushed 10 ft away.

**Rock (Throw):** +8 to hit, range 60/120 ft, 2d10+5 bludgeoning (~16 avg). On hit, target DC 14 STR save or knocked prone.

**Root Eruption (Recharge 5-6):** Roots tear through the ground in a 20 ft radius centered on the guardian. All creatures in the area make DC 15 STR save. Fail: 2d8 piercing + restrained. Success: half, not restrained. Restrained creatures can use their action to make DC 14 STR check to free themselves.

**Lightning Absorption:** Lightning damage is absorbed — heals the guardian instead.

**Blood Root Regeneration:** Regains 15 HP at the start of each turn if within 10 ft of Blood Root.

## Behavior

- Opens with Root Eruption if possible — pin ranged attackers before they can spread out
- Uses Rock to target anyone fleeing or staying at range
- Hammer keeps melee fighters on the ground — knocked prone, pushed back, repeat
- Uses reach to punish anyone who tries to back off
- Ignores pain — no morale, no retreat
- Does NOT pursue beyond the garden perimeter
- Returns dormant if all intruders leave the garden

## Ending the Fight

| Method | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Kill it | Works but angers Pipe — and the garden slowly dies |
| DC 16 Persuasion (with Pipe's help) | Pipe calls it off — it sinks back into the earth |
| [[Selune]] (Druid) DC 14 Nature | Communicate peaceful intentions directly — it pauses |

## Consequences

**If defeated:**
- Garden withers over the following days
- Pipe weeps: "You've killed the last piece of her."
- Party gets Blood Root but at a cost

**If calmed:**
- Guardian retreats into the earth
- Pipe trusts the party
- May share more secrets
