# Combat: Frost Wolf Pack

**Trigger:** Party camps, rests, or gets cornered.

## Environment

- Deep snow (difficult terrain)
- Falling snow provides light obscurement
- Icy rocks can be pushed (DC 14 Athletics) for 2d6 bludgeoning against wolves

## Creatures

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Winter Wolf | 1 | 75 | 13 | +6 | 2d6+4 piercing + 1d6 cold | Cold Breath, Pack Tactics, immune cold |
| Dire Wolf | 3 | 37 | 14 | +5 | 2d6+3 piercing | Knockdown, Pack Tactics |

## Winter Wolf

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 7 (-2) | 12 (+1) | 8 (-1) |

- **Speed:** 50 ft
- **Bite:** Knocks prone (DC 14 STR)
- **Cold Breath (Recharge 5-6):** 15 ft cone, DC 12 CON, 4d8 cold (half on save)
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage if ally within 5 ft

**Behavior:** Opens with Cold Breath, targets prone creatures. Below 20 HP, howls (alerts wolves in 1 mile) and flees. Won't abandon dire wolves.

## Dire Wolf

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 17 (+3) | 15 (+2) | 15 (+2) | 3 (-4) | 12 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

- **Speed:** 50 ft
- **Bite:** Knocks prone (DC 13 STR)
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage if ally within 5 ft

**Behavior:** Circle and flank, target prone creatures. Flee if Winter Wolf dies and two dire wolves down.

## Loot

- Winter Wolf pelt (50gp, cold-resistant cloak material)
- Wolf teeth (spell components)
