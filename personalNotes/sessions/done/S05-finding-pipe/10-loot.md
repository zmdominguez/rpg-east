## Maren's Pendant

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

A silver pendant containing a dried Blood Root flower.

- Advantage on saving throws against disease
- Once per long rest, cast *Lesser Restoration* without a spell slot

## The Rootmother's Legacy (Hidden in Shrine)

**Location:** Beneath a loose stone marked with the Rootmother's symbol.

**Trigger:** Party calms the [[08-combat-root-guardian|Root Guardian]] or earns [[Pipe Ashford]]'s trust.

> "The Rootmother left gifts for those who would protect the balance. But she only left one for each generation. You must choose."

**The party may take ONLY ONE. The others crumble to dust.**

### 1. Whisperwind Quill (for [[Aeris Whisperwind]])

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a bard)*

A white feather quill that writes with silver ink from nowhere.

- Write a message (up to 25 words) that floats to a named creature within 1 mile and whispers only to them. Fades after. (1/long rest)
- Advantage on Performance checks when composing music/poetry
- Hums softly when someone nearby is lying

### 2. Stoneheart Gauntlet (for Kass)

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

A leather gauntlet reinforced with mountain stone, warm to touch.

- Once per long rest, when damage would drop you to 0 HP, use reaction to drop to 1 HP instead
- Advantage on saves against being moved (shoved, pushed, etc.)
- +1 to Athletics checks

### 3. Pactbreaker Shard (for [[Mirr]])

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a warlock)*

A jagged obsidian shard pulsing with faint purple light, worn as amulet.

- When dealing damage with warlock spell or Eldritch Blast, deal extra 1d6 force damage. (1/short or long rest)
- Once per long rest, reroll a failed Wisdom saving throw
- Grows slightly colder when extraplanar entities within 60 feet

### 4. Moonpetal Circlet (for [[Selune]])

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a druid)*

A delicate silver circlet woven with preserved white petals that never wilt.

- Under moonlight, regain one expended spell slot (2nd level or lower) on short rest. (1/long rest)
- Cast *Speak with Plants* 1/long rest without spell slot
- Beasts have disadvantage on first attack against you each combat

## DM Guidance: The Choice

Let the players debate. This is a character moment:

- Who needs it most?
- Who would the Rootmother have wanted to receive it?
- Does the party prioritize utility, sentiment, or fairness?

**If they cannot decide:**
> Pipe: "The Rootmother valued those who gave to the land more than they took. Who among you fits that?"
