# Stage 3: The Rootmother's Garden

## Read Aloud

> Hidden behind a natural stone arch, you find it: a garden that shouldn't exist. Blood Root grows in perfect spiraling rows, more vibrant than any you've seen. Ancient stone pillars mark the perimeter, carved with symbols of roots and rain. At the center stands a crumbling shrine, and beside it - a simple shelter made of canvas and bone.

> A woman with sun-bleached red hair steps out, a wooden staff in her hand. She doesn't run. She looks at you like she's been expecting visitors for a very long time.
