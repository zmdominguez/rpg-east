# The Truth: Pipe's Discovery

## What Pipe Found

When her mother died, [[Pipe Ashford]] sought answers. She tracked Blood Root's origins to these peaks and discovered the **Rootmother's Garden** - an ancient druidic site.

## The Carvings Reveal

- Blood Root was cultivated by a druidic circle centuries ago
- It was meant to heal the land, not people
- The "Rootmother" was their leader - bound her life force to the garden
- When she died, the Blood Root began spreading beyond the peaks
- The Owl Cult are descendants of this circle, but they've forgotten the true purpose
- Long time ago this garden was on the same level as the city of [[Thornhaven]] which didn't existed on time, but something push it up making the peak grow under the garden to isolate it

## Blood Root's True Nature

- Not just a healing herb - it's a magical conduit
- **Amplifies** whatever is "fed" to it:
  - Healing intention = healing
  - Dark magic = amplification
- Someone using it for dark purposes would grow very powerful (foreshadowing [[the-web-of-velthara|Velthara]])
- The witch who taught Pipe's mother was the last person who knew this

## Pipe's Choice

She stayed to protect the garden. She believes if the wrong people learn its location, they'll exploit it.

## Her Concerns About Returning

> "[[Eman]] wants to marry me. I know why. It's not love - it's politics."

> "If I go back, I lose my freedom. But if I stay, my father dies thinking I abandoned him."

> "The garden needs a guardian. The Rootmother's magic is fading. Someone has to take her place."
