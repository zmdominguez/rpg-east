# Combat: The Plateau Hunter (Desert Yeti)

A **Desert Yeti** stalks the sunbleached plateau, a bleached-fur monstrosity that hunts in the dry heat instead of snow.

**Trigger:** The party moves across the open plateau. The yeti emerges from behind a cluster of pale rock formations — its camouflage breaks only when it charges.

## Creatures

| Creature | Count | HP | AC | ATK | DMG | Notes |
|----------|-------|----|----|-----|-----|-------|
| Desert Yeti | 1 | 51 | 12 | +6 | 1d6+4 slash | Multiattack, Searing Gaze (Rech 6), Desert Camouflage |

## Desert Yeti Stats

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 8 (-1) | 13 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

- **Speed:** 40 ft
- **Size:** Large
- **Resistances:** Fire
- **Vulnerability:** Cold
- **Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 13

**Multiattack:** Two claw attacks.

**Claw:** +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1d6+4 slashing. If the target is Surprised, also deals 1d6 fire damage (the scorching heat radiating from its body).

**Searing Gaze (Recharge 6):** The yeti fixes its burning gaze on one creature within 30 ft. DC 13 Constitution save. On failure: 3d6 fire damage and blinded until the end of their next turn (the heat shimmer overwhelms their vision). On success: half damage, not blinded.

**Keen Smell:** Advantage on Perception checks that rely on smell.

**Desert Camouflage:** Advantage on Stealth checks in arid or rocky terrain.

## Behavior

- Lurks behind pale rock formations, nearly invisible until it moves
- Opens with Searing Gaze if it gets the drop on the party (surprise round)
- Closes to melee immediately — not a ranged fighter
- Targets the smallest or most isolated party member
- Fights to the death; this is its territory

## Investigation Note

DC 13 Nature: The creature is a rare high-altitude adaptation — a desert yeti, found only above the cloud line where the sun blazes unfiltered. Cold-blooded, in a sense. A flask of water or any cold-damage spell hits it hard.

## Loot

- Desert Yeti hide (can be fashioned into sun-resistant leather — 20gp as trade goods, or taken to a tanner)
- Four large claws (alchemical component, 8gp each)
- **Bones of an old victim nearby:**
  - 30gp in mixed coins
  - Carved bone amulet (15gp)
  - Torn cloth with Blood Root embroidery
