# Resolution Options

The party can influence what [[Pipe Ashford]] decides.

## Option 1: Return to Thornhaven

- Pipe reunites with [[Roderick Ashford|her father]]
- [[Eman]]'s marriage proposal becomes a campaign thread
- The garden loses its guardian (future consequences?)

**Reward:** Full payment from Eman + Roderick's gratitude

## Option 2: Stay at the Garden

- Pipe remains as the new guardian
- Party lies to [[Eman]] (creates tension)
- [[Roderick Ashford]] can visit if told the truth

**Reward:** Pipe teaches them about Blood Root (advantage on checks involving Blood Root)

## Option 3: Expose Eman's Motives

- Party tells Pipe (and potentially Thornhaven) why [[Eman]] really wants to find her
- Political fallout for Eman
- Owl Cult connection becomes relevant

**Reward:** Roderick's heirloom + potential ally in Pipe

## Competing Mercenaries (Optional)

[[Eman]] mentioned other mercenaries. If desired:
- Arrive at the garden after the party
- Create tension: do they fight for the "prize"?
- Could be Cross Bones Inc members or new NPCs

## Character Moments

- **[[Mirr]]:** Goliath connection to [[Hilda]] at [[Venn]]'s cabin
- **[[Selune]]:** Druidic connection to the garden, can communicate with guardian
- **[[Aeris Whisperwind]]:** Social encounters with Pipe, emotional weight of the choice
- **Kass:** Flexible based on class/abilities

## If Things Go Sideways

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Party attacks Pipe | She's a capable survivor (Scout stat block + druid cantrips) |
| Party brings mercenaries | Combat or standoff |
| Party tells Eman about the garden | She wants to control it - future conflict |
