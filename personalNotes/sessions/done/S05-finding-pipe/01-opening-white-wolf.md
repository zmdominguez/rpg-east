# Opening: The White Wolf

The wolf that watched them is the **Winter Wolf** - intelligent, territorial. It's been observing since they entered its territory.

## DM Options

Choose one approach:

### Option 1: Immediate Attack
The wolf pack ambushes as the party breaks camp.

### Option 2: Stalking (Recommended)
The wolf follows at a distance, attacks when party is vulnerable. Build tension - let them feel hunted.

### Option 3: Territorial Warning
Wolf howls, pack surrounds but doesn't attack unless provoked.

## Recommendation

Option 2 or 3 - build tension first. The combat happens later via [[02-combat-frost-wolves]].
