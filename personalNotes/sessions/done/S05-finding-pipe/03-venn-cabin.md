# Venn's Cabin (Optional Return)

If the party knocks again or tries to get [[Venn]]'s help.

## Venn's Response

Still reluctant. Will give minimal info through the door:

> "Cloudbreak Pass is half a day north. Follow the channel markers."

> "Don't go past the clouds unless you want to die of thirst up there."

> "The woman I saw was near the old stone arch. That's all I know."

## The Giant Woman: Hilda

[[Hilda]] is Venn's girlfriend - a Goliath who lives with him secretly. Venn is embarrassed about having a personal life.

**Opening the Door:**
- DC 18 Persuasion, OR
- [[Mirr]] (also Goliath) speaks to her directly

## What Hilda Knows

- She's seen lights above the clouds at night
- "Something lives up there. Not animals. Someone tends a fire."
- She won't go with them but wishes them luck
