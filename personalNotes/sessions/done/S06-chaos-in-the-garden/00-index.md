# Session S06: Chaos in the Garden

**Party Level:** 5
**Type:** Combat-heavy (multi-faction brawl)
**Location:** Rootmother's Garden

## Context

S05 ended mid-combat: [[Cross Bones Inc]] ambushed the camp at night, knocked [[Pipe Ashford]] unconscious, set the tents on fire, and deliberately lured a Desert Yeti into the garden for chaos. The Root Guardian animated to defend Pipe. The party wakes up inside this fight.

## The Teleportation Device (Key Event)

Cross Bones has a one-time teleportation device. **At the start of Jynx's Turn 4**, it activates automatically (malfunction). Jynx vanishes with Pipe. Nyra is left behind holding the book.

> *"A device on Jynx's belt flares with crackling blue light — CRACK — a flash of arcane energy, and Jynx vanishes, taking Pipe with them. The garden goes silent for one heartbeat."*

Nyra on her Turn 4: *"The device activated too early — you absolute idiot!"*

## Forcing Party Engagement

The party might try to watch from the sidelines. Use these pressure valves:
- **Desert Yeti** is a wild animal — if Cross Bones gets far enough away, it will pivot to the nearest creature (the party)
- **Root Guardian** cannot distinguish allies from enemies after Turn 3 if nobody is actively helping Pipe — it starts treating everyone near the garden as a threat
- **Nyra's panic (Turn 4+)** creates an obvious social hook: she has information about Pipe's location and will trade it for protection
