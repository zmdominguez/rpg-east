# Jinx — Combat Reference

**Role:** Cross Bones lead operative. Carries the teleportation device. Goal: escape with Pipe through the garden exit before anyone can stop her.
**Personality:** Precise, quiet, ruthless when threatened. Uses magic like a scalpel — exactly as much as needed. See [[Jinx]] for full sheet.

## Teleportation Device

One-use. Fixed anchor at an unknown city location. **Activates automatically at the start of Jinx's Turn 4** (malfunction). Jinx disappears with Pipe. Nyra is left behind.

## Turn-by-Turn

**Turn 1 (Init 19):**
Move to Pipe → pick up (object interaction while moving, movement halved to 15ft).
Bonus action: **Quickened Misty Step** (2 SP) → teleport 30ft toward exit carrying Pipe. Total escape distance this turn: ~45ft.
Action: **Fire Bolt** on Yeti (+7, 2d10 fire, avg 11). Discourage pursuit.
*No words. Just moves.*

**Turn 2 (Init 19):**
Movement 15ft (halved, carrying Pipe).
Reaction if Yeti attacks: **Shield** (+5 AC, 1st level slot) — she cannot afford to take heavy hits.
Action: **Scorching Ray** (2nd level, 3 rays, +7 each, 2d6+4 fire per ray) on Yeti if still pursuing. First ray adds Elemental Affinity (+4). Total avg: ~25 fire damage.
OR if Yeti is out of range: **Sorcerous Burst** on Guardian to slow it down.

**Turn 3 (Init 19):**
Bonus action: **Necrotic Shroud** (Fallen Aasimar, 1/LR). Dark wings tear through her coat. Eyes go black.
All creatures within 10ft: **DC 15 CHA save or frightened** until end of their next turn.
*This likely catches the Yeti and possibly the Guardian. Frightened creatures can't move toward the source.*
Movement 15ft toward exit.
Action: **Sorcerous Burst** (+7, 2d8+4 fire, range 120ft, extra die on max) + extra **+6 necrotic** from Shroud on the hit target.
*"Time's up."*

**Turn 4 (Start of Turn):**
> Device activates. Jinx and Pipe vanish.

## Flee/Surrender Condition

She doesn't surrender. If reduced to 0 HP before Turn 4, use Fireball as a dying action centered on herself (she'd rather blow the garden up than get captured). If somehow captured, she says nothing — not one word.
