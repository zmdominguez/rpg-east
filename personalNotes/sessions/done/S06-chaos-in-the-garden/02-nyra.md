# Nyra — Combat Reference

**Role:** Cross Bones operative. Objectives: grab the book, support Jynx, escape. After Turn 4 (Jynx vanishes): survive and negotiate.
**Personality:** Cool under pressure until she isn't. Pragmatic. Will absolutely trade information for safety. See [[Nyra]] for full backstory.

## Stat Block

| HP | AC | Speed | Initiative | Prof |
|----|----|----|--------|------|
| 21 | 16 | 30 ft | +4 | +3 |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) | 6 (-2) | 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 6 (-2) |

**Saves:** DEX +7, INT +6
**Fragile:** 21 HP means one Guardian hit (avg 18) nearly kills her. She CANNOT tank hits.

## Attacks

| Attack | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|--------|-----|--------|-------|
| Dagger | +7 | 1d4+4 piercing + 3d6 SA | Sneak Attack: needs advantage OR ally adj. to target |
| Shortsword | +7 | 1d6+4 piercing + 3d6 SA | |
| Offhand Dagger | +7 | 1d4+4 piercing | Bonus action, no SA |

**Sneak Attack (3d6):** Once per turn. Triggers if she has advantage on the attack OR if an ally is within 5ft of the target.

## Abilities

**Cunning Action:** Bonus action — Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

**Uncanny Dodge:** When attacked by a creature she can see, use reaction to halve incoming damage.

**Evasion:** DEX save for half damage → no damage on success; takes half on fail.

**Wayfarer Features:** Enhanced movement abilities (homebrew — treat as extra 10ft speed in open terrain or advantage on Athletics checks to climb/jump).

**Cloak of Manta:** Swim 15ft, breathe underwater 5 minutes. (Irrelevant in garden combat but she has it.)

## Turn Suggestions

**Turn 1:** Cunning Action Dash (bonus action) → grab the book (object interaction). Move toward Jynx. If in range: Dagger attack on Guardian (SA if Snag is adjacent to Guardian). Avg 18 damage with SA.

**Turn 2:** Has the book. Stay near Jynx. If Guardian is threatening: Disengage (bonus action) + move away. Throw a dagger at whoever blocks the path.

**Turn 3:** Jynx is close to escape. Nyra covers them. Sneak Attack on whatever is chasing Jynx.

**Turn 4:** JYNX VANISHES. Nyra is alone, surrounded.
- Cunning Action Disengage + full Dash (move up to 60ft away from Guardian).
- If party is visible: *"I'll tell you everything! Just get me out of here!"*
- If cornered: *"The device activated too early — you absolute idiot!"* [directed at absent Jynx]

**Turn 5:** Last escape attempt or opening negotiation.
- Offer: *"I know where the device sends them. There's an anchor location. I can tell you."*
- If running: throw a dagger backward while Dashing.

**Turn 6:** If cornered, hands up. She's a survivor — she doesn't die for a job.
- *"Alright. I'm done. What do you want?"*

**Turn 7:** Talks. Freely.
- Confirms Eman hired Cross Bones.
- *"Jynx... I don't think Jynx is just working for Eman. There's someone above him."*
- *"The device has a return anchor. Somewhere in the city. I don't know where — Jynx never told me."*

**Turn 8:** Prisoner or escaped. Either way, the party has a lead.

## Information Nyra Knows

- Eman hired Cross Bones to retrieve Pipe and the book.
- The teleportation device is a one-use item. The anchor is fixed — Jynx will reappear at a specific location.
- Nyra suspects Jynx is working a second job — someone above Eman. She doesn't know who.
- She joined Cross Bones for the money. She doesn't care about Blood Root.
