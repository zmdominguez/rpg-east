# Desert Yeti — Combat Reference

**Role:** Wild card. Lured into the garden by Cross Bones. Targets whoever disturbed it (Cross Bones scent first), but is a dumb animal and will pivot when they vanish.
**Initiative Roll:** Rolled **14** + 1 = **15**

## Stat Block

| HP | AC | Speed | Initiative | Size |
|----|----|----|--------|------|
| 51 | 12 | 40 ft | 15 | Large |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 8 (-1) | 13 (+1) | 7 (-2) |

**Resistances:** Fire
**Vulnerability:** Cold
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 13

## Attacks

| Attack | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|--------|-----|--------|-------|
| Claw | +6 | 1d6+4 slashing | Extra 1d6 fire if target is Surprised |
| Claw | +6 | 1d6+4 slashing | |

**Multiattack:** Two Claw attacks per action.

**Searing Gaze** (Recharge 6): Range 30 ft. One target, DC 13 CON save.
- Fail: 3d6 fire damage + blinded until end of next turn.
- Success: Half damage, not blinded.

**Keen Smell:** Advantage on Perception checks that rely on smell.

**Desert Camouflage:** Advantage on Stealth checks in arid/rocky terrain. (Less relevant in garden.)

## Flee Condition

Flees when below **25 HP** (moves toward garden edge, disengages if possible). Has no loyalty to anyone — pure survival instinct.

## Turn-by-Turn

**Turn 1 (Init 15, after Nyra):**
Cross Bones disturbed it. Jynx is the strongest scent. Charge Jynx: full move (40ft) + 2 Claw attacks on Jynx.
- Claw +6, 1d6+4 (avg 7) × 2

**Turn 2:**
Jynx is fleeing. Still within 40ft? Continue 2 Claws on Jynx.
Searing Gaze recharge check (needs 6 on d6): if recharged, use on Jynx instead of claws — DC 13 CON, 3d6 fire + blind.
If Jynx moved out of range: pivot to Snag (closest alternative).

**Turn 3:**
If Jynx is out of 40ft range, Snag is now the target. 2 Claws on Snag.
OR: Root Guardian just hit the Yeti with something (area attack or rock throw). If so, Yeti is angry at the Guardian — pivots to it for 2 Claws.
Searing Gaze check if not yet used.

**Turn 4:**
Jynx VANISHES. The Yeti sniffs the air — confused. Roll d6 for new target:
- **1–2:** Nyra (panicking, easiest target)
- **3–4:** Snag (still fighting nearby)
- **5–6:** Root Guardian (it's been a huge presence all fight)
2 Claw attacks on chosen target.

**Turn 5:**
Check HP. If at or below 25: Yeti tries to flee. Uses full movement toward garden edge. If something is adjacent, Disengage first; otherwise just runs (triggering opportunity attacks).
If above 25 HP: continues attacking current target. Searing Gaze recharge check.

**Turn 6:**
If fleeing: exits garden. Remove from tracker.
If still fighting: one last Searing Gaze attempt (recharge 6) on Guardian — DC 13 CON vs Guardian's +8 = almost always saves, but half fire damage gets through. Then 2 Claws on nearest.

**Turn 7:**
Either gone or near dead. If cornered (nowhere to flee), it's desperate: Reckless fighting — attack with advantage (no mechanical rule, just narrative — it's panicking).

**Turn 8:**
Fled or dead.
