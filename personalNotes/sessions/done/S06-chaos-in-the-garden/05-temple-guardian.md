# Root Guardian — Combat Reference

**Role:** Temple defender. Priority order: (1) protect Pipe, (2) protect the book, (3) destroy threats to the garden.
**Threat Detection:** Cannot distinguish party from attackers. If party members don't actively help Pipe by Turn 3, the Guardian considers them enemies of the garden.
**Initiative Roll:** Rolled **8** − 2 = **6**

## Stat Block

| HP | AC | Speed | Initiative | Size |
|----|----|----|--------|------|
| 152 | 16 | 20 ft | 6 | Huge |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 21 (+5) | 6 (-2) | 20 (+5) | 3 (-4) | 10 (+0) | 3 (-4) |

**Resistances:** Cold, Fire
**Immunity:** Lightning (absorbed as healing)
**Condition Immunity:** Blinded, Deafened, Exhaustion, Prone
**Blood Root Regeneration:** Regains **15 HP** at the start of each turn if within 10ft of the Blood Root.

## Attacks

| Attack | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|--------|-----|--------|-------|
| Hammer Strike | +8 | 2d12+5 bludgeoning (avg 18) | Reach 10ft. DC 15 STR or prone + pushed 10ft |
| Rock Throw | +8 | 2d10+5 bludgeoning (avg 16) | Range 60/120. DC 14 STR or prone |

**Multiattack:** One Hammer Strike + one Rock Throw per action.

**Root Eruption** (Recharge 5–6): 20ft radius centered on target point. DC 15 STR save.
- Fail: 2d8 piercing + restrained.
- Success: Half damage, not restrained.
*Roll recharge at the start of each turn. On 5 or 6: available.*

## Turn-by-Turn

**Turn 1 (Init 6):**
Blood Root Regen (+15 HP if in range — probably not first turn, Guardian just animated).
Jynx has Pipe. That's the threat. Move toward Jynx (20ft).
Multiattack: Hammer on Jynx (+8, 2d12+5, DC 15 STR or prone+pushed). Rock Throw at Nyra (+8, 2d10+5, DC 14 STR or prone).

**Turn 2:**
Blood Root Regen (+15 HP).
Root Eruption recharge check (5–6): if yes, use centered on Jynx's location — hits Jynx, possibly Yeti and others nearby.
Otherwise: Multiattack on Jynx (Hammer) + Nyra (Rock Throw).
If Snag keeps hitting it: one Hammer on Snag to knock him prone, then continue on Jynx.

**Turn 3:**
Blood Root Regen.
Jynx is close to exit. Guardian increases urgency. Charges toward Jynx.
Root Eruption recharge check. If available: centered near the exit to block escape.
Otherwise: Hammer on Jynx. Rock Throw at Nyra.
**Threat Detection trigger:** If any party member is just watching, Guardian treats them as an enemy. Rock Throw on the most passive-looking player character.

**Turn 4:**
Blood Root Regen.
PIPE VANISHES at the start of Jynx's turn. The Guardian stops mid-action.

> *"The guardian lets out a grinding howl — stone grinding against stone, roots cracking under pressure — an agonized sound. It turns, searching, and finds: Nyra, holding the book."*

New priority: the book. Charges Nyra.
Rock Throw at Nyra if she's fled far. Hammer if she's within 10ft.
Root Eruption if available: center on Nyra.

**Turn 5:**
Blood Root Regen.
Hunts Nyra relentlessly. If Nyra is within reach: Hammer + push (DC 15 STR or prone + 10ft).
Rock Throw at anything in the way.
If the Yeti is between Guardian and Nyra: Hammer on Yeti first, then throw Rock at Nyra.

**Turn 6:**
Blood Root Regen.
If Nyra dropped the book: Guardian stops. Stands over the book. Only attacks if something approaches within 10ft.
If Nyra still has the book and is fleeing: Root Eruption if recharged on the path ahead of her (restrained = she stops).
Otherwise: Hammer on whoever is nearest.

**Turn 7:**
Blood Root Regen.
If the book is secure: Guardian returns to Blood Root position. Goes dormant. Will attack if approached.
If combat is ongoing: Root Eruption on any cluster. Then Multiattack on the nearest threat.

**Turn 8:**
Blood Root Regen.
Combat resolution. If book is in Guardian's possession or at Blood Root: Guardian is motionless, watching.
If the book is still in play: one final Root Eruption if available.

## Effective HP Note

With Blood Root Regen at 15 HP/turn and 152 base HP, the Guardian effectively has ~270 HP over an 8-turn fight if it stays near the Blood Root. Don't move it far from the Blood Root unless dramatically necessary.
