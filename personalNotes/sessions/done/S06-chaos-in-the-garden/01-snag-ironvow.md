# Snag Ironvow — Combat Reference

**Role:** Cross Bones muscle. Goblin Fighter 5 (Champion). Absolutely convinced he is a Paladin.
**Personality:** Heroic speeches before every action. Calls his combat abilities holy gifts. Tries to protect "innocents" even when fighting alongside criminals. Calls himself "Sir Snag." Nobody calls him Sir Snag.

| HP | AC | Speed | Initiative | Prof |

**Saves:** STR +7, CON +6
**Skills:** Athletics +7, Intimidation +4, Perception +3, Religion +2
**Darkvision:** 60 ft
**Passive Perception:** 13

## Attacks

| Attack | Hit | Damage | Notes |
|--------|-----|--------|-------|
| Warhammer | +7 | 1d8+4 bludgeoning | Versatile: 1d10+4 two-handed |
| Shield Bash | +7 | 1d4+4 bludgeoning | Improvised, bonus action |

**Extra Attack:** Two attacks per Attack action.

## Abilities

**Action Surge** (1/short rest) — "The Holy One demands more!": Take one additional action on your turn. Use when below 30 HP or when one more action could drop an enemy.

**Second Wind** (1/short rest) — "The Holy One breathes through me!": Bonus action, regain 1d10+5 HP (avg 10). Use when below 20 HP.

**Indomitable** (1/long rest): Reroll one failed saving throw.

**Improved Critical (Champion):** Crits on 19–20.

**Fury of the Small (Goblin):** Once per turn when hitting a creature larger than Snag, deal +5 psychic damage.

**Nimble Escape (Goblin):** Disengage or Hide as bonus action (replaces Second Wind's bonus action on that turn if both needed).

## Equipment

Chain Mail (16) + Shield (+2) + Defense Fighting Style (+1) = AC 19
Warhammer, two throwing hammers (range 20/60, +7, 1d4+4)
Carved bone relic on a cord — he calls it a token of the Holy One and keeps it face-down against his chest. He has never explained what it depicts and cannot be made to look at it.

## Turn Suggestions

**Turn 1:** Dramatic entrance speech + move toward Guardian + 2 Warhammer attacks.
*"CITIZENS! Fear not! Sir Snag Ironvow, Paladin of the Holy One, has ARRIVED!"*

**Turn 2:** Continue attacking Guardian. If below 20 HP, Second Wind as bonus action.
*"Your stone heart cannot withstand the light of the Holy One!"*

**Turn 3:** Yeti may pivot to Snag. Attack whoever is lower HP (Guardian or Yeti). If Yeti is close, 2 attacks on Yeti.

**Turn 4:** Jynx vanishes. Snag is shocked and inspired. Action Surge: 4 total attacks, splitting between Guardian and Yeti or dumping all on one.
*"The heavens themselves have intervened! The Holy One's purpose is clear!"*

**Turn 5:** Pursues Nyra if she's fleeing. Tries to block her escape. *"You will face justice, criminal!"*

**Turn 6:** If Guardian is near-dead, declares victory and pivots to Nyra interrogation.

**Turn 7:** Attempts to be the "heroic voice of reason" with Nyra. Actually useful for intimidation.
*"I offer you a sacred accord: speak the truth, and Sir Snag grants you mercy!"*

**Turn 8:** If still standing, offers to join the party to find Pipe.
*"The Holy One has led me here for a reason. Your quest is now my quest."*

## Flee/Surrender Condition

Drops to 0 HP dramatically: *"The Holy... Hammer... calls me home..."* — then wakes up immediately at 1 HP from Indomitable (dramatic reroll), if not already used.

If captured: cooperates immediately and tries to convince captors he was "working undercover all along."
