# Veiled Peaks — DM Secrets

Confirmed discoveries (after S05):

- **Cloudbreak Pass:** Thermal boundary where snow and desert coexist side by side — a natural phenomenon, not magical
- **Sunbleached Plateau:** Desert environment above the clouds; thin air causes exhaustion on strenuous activity; long rests are reduced unless you make a Constitution save
- **Rootmother's Garden:** Confirmed location — hidden behind a stone arch on the plateau; spiral-planted Blood Root beds, ancient stone pillars with root/rain carvings, crumbling shrine, Pipe's canvas shelter
- **Water source:** The irrigation channels feeding Thornhaven originate in the peaks; Venn maintains them but his disappearance (S05) leaves their status uncertain

## Open Secrets

- Old stories speak of the garden being tended by "no one" — this was Pipe, but nobody in Thornhaven knew
- Venn's girlfriend [[Hilda]] (Goliath) lives secretly with him in his cabin; she has seen lights above the clouds at night and knows "something lives up there" — potentially a witness to Pipe's presence before the party found her
- **Venn's disappearance** during S05 is unexplained — no tracks, no sign; Hilda may have answers or may have vanished too

## Aeldris Vorn's Tower

Above the snow line in the Veiled Peaks — location unknown to the party. Contains:
- The controlled dispersal method for Gorethis's fragments (endgame key)
- Full Verdant Cycle mechanics documentation

The Rootmother's Garden is at the base of the ascent to wherever the tower sits. Players are now geographically close to it without knowing.
