# Graveyard Shortsword - DM Notes

## The Secret

The shortsword is not magical, but it carries a connection to Qualam's past.

## The Truth

This blade once belonged to a member of Qualam's family - perhaps a relative he never knew, or one he's long forgotten. The grave robbers stole it from a burial site, unknowingly taking an heirloom meant to be laid to rest with its owner.

## Why It Feels Right

The blade was crafted by elven smiths for Qualam's bloodline. Though mundane, it was made with measurements and balance suited to his family's fighting style. The "attunement" is simply muscle memory - the sword feels right because it was made for hands like his.

## Possible Revelations

1. **Finding the grave it was stolen from** - Could reveal family connection
2. **An NPC recognizes the blade** - Someone who knew the previous owner
3. **Inscriptions appear** - Under moonlight, faded elven runes become visible on the blade
4. **Dreams** - Qualam begins seeing glimpses of the sword's history

## Story Hooks

- Who was buried with this sword?
- Why was Qualam's relative buried in Thornhaven?
- Is there a living family member who knows the truth?
- The grave robbers - were they targeting this specific grave, or was it random?

## When to Reveal

Wait until Vitor provides Qualam's backstory. The sword's history can then tie into whatever family/origin story he creates.
