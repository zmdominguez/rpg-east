## Description

Mid-thirties. Plain, deliberately so — clothes that don't stand out, hair kept simple. The kind of person a room forgets. Moves through crowds without friction.

[[Aeris Whisperwind]] recognizes her face from Elarion's court. She was a low-ranking attendant — one of the people who arranged logistics for private performances at the high court. She was there the night Thalen performed his last show.

## Notes

- Was present at Thalen Rook's final performance for Elarion's high court
- Left Elarion shortly after Thalen's disappearance — the timing is not a coincidence, but her reasons are her own
- Is now in Thornhaven. Why? DM to decide.
- When she sees [[Aeris]] at the [[Cross Bones Pub]], she watches the full performance before leaving. She is not running — she is deciding.

## What She Knows (DM decides how much)

Options — pick one or combine:
- She witnessed what actually happened to Thalen that night and has been carrying it alone since
- She was sent by someone in Elarion to locate Aeris — but has had second thoughts about the mission
- She knew Thalen personally (beyond professional contact) and has her own reasons for grief
- She knows Thalen is alive and knows where he is

## What She Wants

Unknown until the DM decides her role. She is not immediately dangerous. She may be an ally, a complication, or both.

## DM Notes

- First appearance: S07, evening at the [[Cross Bones Pub]] — Aeris sees her face in the crowd, she leaves before being approached
- Do not resolve on first appearance. She is a thread.
- Her next move: she will find a way to approach Aeris privately, when the rest of the party is not present
- Her opening line (when she finally speaks): something that proves she knows who Aeris is — not a name, but a detail only someone at that court performance would know
