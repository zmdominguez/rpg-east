## Description

Old. Very old for a human — late seventies, still moving under his own power but slowly. Weathered skin, watery grey eyes that don't miss much. Wears the plain Scarsith grey with no adornment. Rarely speaks in group settings. Sits in corners and watches.

Other priests treat him with quiet deference but rarely seek him out. He has been at the temple longer than anyone can remember.

## Notes

- One of the oldest active members of the Scarsith congregation
- Has access to the temple's oldest records — pre-Thornhaven documents preserved in the lower archive
- Found references to the **silver eclipse** symbol in those records decades ago. Has been waiting since then for someone who would recognize the question.
- Knew a young woman years ago who bore some connection to the symbol. He doesn't know her name — she passed through briefly and never returned.
- His question to [[Selune]]: *"The silver eclipse. Where did you see it?"* — is the first thing he has said about this in twenty years.

## What He Knows

- The silver eclipse is the mark of a druidic circle that predates Thornhaven's founding — older than the [[Owl Cult]], possibly older than the settlement itself.
- The circle was connected to the root network in ways that went beyond typical druidic practice.
- He believes [[Selune]]'s origin is tied to this circle — not randomness, not coincidence.
- He does not have the full answer. He has the oldest thread.

## What He Wants

Nothing for himself. He is old and close to Scarsith's embrace. He wants the question answered before he dies — and he wants whoever carries the answer to understand what it means.

He will not approach the party through [[Nell]]. This is not church business. It is his own.

## DM Notes

- Introduce only when [[Selune]] is present at the [[Scarsith Temple]]
- He connects to Selune's backstory arc — the "cold hands, chanting voices, silver eclipse carved in stone"
- Do not resolve his thread quickly — he is a doorway, not an answer
- He could eventually lead the party to the lower temple archive, which has records that cross-reference the [[Owl Cult]]'s own history
