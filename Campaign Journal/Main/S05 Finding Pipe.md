## 💡Key Learnings

- [[Venn]] disappeared overnight without a trace
- [[Cloudbreak Pass]] is a natural thermal boundary — snow and desert exist side by side
- [[Pipe Ashford]] is alive and living as the Rootmother's Garden guardian
- Pipe refuses to return to [[Thornhaven]] — the garden must have a guardian
- [[Eman]]'s love for Pipe was never real — she wants power
- [[Blood root]] heals the land, not people, and amplifies whatever it touches — dangerous in the wrong hands
- Pipe holds a book with all her Blood Root research and won't share it
- [[Kass]] stole some Blood Root during the night
- [[Selune]] carries a letter from Pipe to deliver to [[Roderick Ashford]]
- [[Cross Bones Inc]] are the other mercenaries hired by Eman — they ambushed the party at night
- Cross Bones knocked out Pipe and set the tents on fire
- The Root Guardian animated to defend Pipe
- Cross Bones lured a Desert Yeti into the garden


## 🤝 Who Did We Meet?

[[Pipe Ashford]] - Found alive in the Rootmother's Garden. Serves as the garden's guardian. Refused to return home. Deeply emotional about her father but her duty comes first.


## 📝 Session Notes

- Woke to find [[Venn]] gone — no tracks, no sign
- Combat: six-legged wolf in the mist
	- [[Kass]] was separated in the fog, rejoined mid-fight
- Still in mist — [[Kass]] spotted a wolf pack ahead on their path
	- [[Selune]] stopped and searched for clues
	- Found an owl eating a desert lizard (impossible in snow)
	- Treated it as an omen — followed the owl
- Owl led them to [[Cloudbreak Pass]]
	- [[Mirr]] expected a portal; it was a natural thermal wall
	- Snow and desert coexist on either side
- Crossed into the desert — harsh heat dealt damage over time
- [[Mirr]] recalled [[Venn]]'s words about an arch; [[Kass]] spotted it from a distance
- Entered the [[Rootmother's Garden]] and found [[Pipe Ashford]]
- After a short rest, questioned Pipe:
	- She refused to return — garden needs a guardian
	- Emotional about [[Roderick Ashford]] (her father) but duty is higher
	- [[Eman]]'s love was never real — she wants power
	- [[Blood root]] heals land, not people; amplifies everything — cannot fall into wrong hands
	- Pipe would not share her research book
- During the conversation:
	- [[Kass]] slipped away ("poop break") and stole Blood Root
	- [[Mirr]] and [[Selune]] ended up on opposing sides of the debate
- [[Selune]] accepted a letter from Pipe addressed to [[Roderick Ashford]]
- Party decided to sleep the night and leave at dawn
- **Night ambush:**
	- [[Mirr]]'s alarm trap triggered
	- [[Cross Bones Inc]] knocked out Pipe and set tents on fire
	- Root Guardian statue animated to defend Pipe
	- Cross Bones lured a Desert Yeti into the garden
- Session ended mid-combat
