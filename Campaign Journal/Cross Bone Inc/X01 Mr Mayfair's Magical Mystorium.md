## 🤝 Who Did We Meet? 

**[[Toodle]]** - A construct maze companion

## 📝 Session Notes
- A world-famous fair appeared overnight just outside the city
	- The Cross Bones party decided to visit to celebrate [[Jinx]]'s birthday
	- After a slight misunderstanding, [[Reverend Aldric]] bought entrance tickets for everyone, including his zombies and skeletons
	- [[Reverend Aldric]] played the strongman game
		- Won a pink studded bracelet
		- Gifted it to one of his zombies
	- [[Nyra]] played the ring toss game
		- Won a fake ring with set with a cloudy gem 
	- [[Selune]] played the shell-and-cups game
		- Won a set of playing cards, missing the King of Hearts
		- Noticed the attendant hiding the shell in his sleeve
		- Intimidated the attendant away and he quit his job and ran away
		- Also now owns the shell-and-cups game
	- Everyone had a fun time
		- [[Nyra]] got fairy floss and tiger face paint
	- An announcement over the PA said whoever figures out the maze gets a chance to win a meet-and-greet with [[Mr Mayfair]]
		- [[Jinx]] wanted to meet [[Mr Mayfair]] **AND*** also ride the dragon coaster
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] decreed the party can only do one and then it's time to go home
		- Another fair-goer mentioned that there are rumours [[Mr Mayfair]]'s office is in the middle of the fairgrounds
		- It just so happens that the maze is also set in the middle of the fairgrounds
- [[Reverend Aldric]] notices a little girl who looked like his deceased daughter
- The party heads towards the maze
	- It is weirdly much quieter here
	- The little girl is now standing near the entrance to the maze
- Party enters the maze
	- Initially looks like a classic mirror maze
	- [[Toodle]] appears
		- Introduces herself as a "maze companion"
		- Appears as a floating lantern
	- First room
		- Everyone got turned to toddlers
		- The room is filled with classic baby toys -- dolls and dollhouses, building blocks, etc
		- There is a door on one side of the room but the handle is too high for the baby party to reach
		- There was a lot of crying and some temper tantrums as the baby party try to figure out how to get out
		- The baby party tries to build a human pyramid, with [[Nyra]] at the top
			- They were not able to open the door, but learned the door is not locked
		- The baby party tries to build a skeleton-zombie-human pyramid, with [[Nyra]] at the top
			- It was pretty gross climbing onto a zombie
			- They successfully opened the door
	- Second room
		- Everyone is back to their normal, present ages
		- The room is lushly and tastefully decorated -- a table filled with food and drink, comfortable couches and beds, etc
		- There is a door on the far end of the room
		- Doppelgangers of each person appears
			- Seem to mirror everything the party does or says
			- Their words are not echoes, but actually appear to say them at the same time as the actual person
			- They appear to be corporeal (continuing to move forward, you will eventually bump into them)
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] tries to trick his doppelganger by touching himself
			- Walked up to it until they are face-to-face
			- He then walked in a semi-circle until he is on the other side of the room
			- This move is now known as "The Tango"
			- His doppelganger flees out of the room
		- The rest of the party decide to The Tango with their own doppelgangers
		- The party all leave the room
	- Third room
		- Everyone comes up to a mirror, not unlike the Mirror of Erised
		- [[Toodle]]'s reflection in the mirror shows her hovering over a huge pile of bones
		- The mirror shows each person's desire
			- [[Reverend Aldric]] sees his family
			- [[Jinx]] sees [[Reverend Aldric]] finally becoming his teacher
			- [[Nyra]] sees herself back in her hometown and not having to run anymore
			- [[Selune]] sees herself with her family enjoying nature together
			- The zombies also see their desires
				- One misses his dog and wants another pink studded bracelet
				- One lives a good life running a shop
				- One wants an eternal rest
		- [[Selune]] and [[Nyra]] were somehow able to snap themselves out of the dream, but [[Jinx]] and [[Reverend Aldric]] are still deeply engrossed
		- [[Selune]] tried to push [[Jinx]] through the mirror
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] prayed to get out of the stupor and his god heard him
		- [[Selune]], [[Reverend Aldric]], and [[Nyra]] stumble through the portal
		- [[Toodle]] bade them farewell
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] asked [[Toodle]] to bury the bones in the town graveyard
	- Fourth room
		- The party stumbles over [[Jinx]], who is caught in a net
		- This room is a small office and man tries to shoo the party out
		- The man turns out to be [[Mr Mayfair]]
			- He has been looking for his daughter
			- Luring people with magic and deception
			- Searched for [[Reverend Aldric]] thinking he "took" his daughter
			- Surprised to hear that [[Toodle]] is still alive, after summoning her 300 years ago
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] is overcome with anger at seeing [[Mr Mayfair]] and attacks him
		- A battle ensues
			- [[Selune]] wildshaped into an ape
			- [[Jinx]] summoned wild magic and somehow pulled [[Toodle]] back into the room
			- [[Toodle]] was distraught at the possibility of having to hurt [[Mr Mayfair]]
			- [[Mr Mayfair]] was eventually defeated
	- After [[Mr Mayfair]] died, the fair and all magic disappeared
		- [[Toodle]] laid in the middle of the fairground, powered down
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] gathered all the bones, and [[Mr Mayfair]]'s body, to be buried
		- [[Jinx]] took [[Toodle]] with him
		- [[Reverend Aldric]] decided to retire from adventuring
