## Description

A stone building on the older edge of [[Aussen]], built in a style that predates the settlement — low arched doorways, walls of fitted basalt block, no mortar. Thelasian construction. The doors are heavy ironwood, sealed with a chain and a wax-stamped lock bearing an administrative mark from the Thornhaven regional authority. The windows are shuttered from the inside.

The building is intact. No signs of collapse or fire. It was closed deliberately, not abandoned in crisis.

A small brass plate is mounted beside the door, weathered but legible:

> *Certified Closed — Regional Authority of Thornhaven — Year of the Hollow Moon*

No explanation. No owner's name.

## History

The workshop operated for at least two generations before its closure, producing ceramics and metalwork using Thelasian craft techniques that predate the founding of [[Thornhaven]]. Its goods were distinctive — identifiable by a maker's mark stamped or fired into every piece. The workshop was a known source for artifact dealers and collectors across the region.

Approximately thirty years ago it was sealed by order of the regional authority. The official record lists the closure as "administrative" with no further detail. The building and its contents have remained untouched since.

## Current State

- Doors chained and wax-sealed — the seal is intact from the outside
- The workshop's maker's mark occasionally appears on goods sold in [[Thornhaven]]'s market — pieces described as coming from the direction of [[Aussen]] and the ruins nearby
- No one in Aussen claims to know why it was sealed or who holds the key

## Notable Events

- [[S07 Return to Thornhaven]] — [[Mirr]] identifies a piece in the Thornhaven morning market bearing the workshop's maker's mark. The stall owner reports it came from a frightened traveler passing through from the direction of the ruins.
