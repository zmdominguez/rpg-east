## Description
Mountain range north of Thornhaven that provides water to the farmlands through irrigation channels. The peaks rise above the clouds into a harsh, dry plateau.

## Key Features
- **Lower slopes:** Cold, snowy, wolf territory

## Key NPCs
- [[Venn]] - Waterkeeper who maintains the irrigation channels

## Notable Locations
- Ice Bridge - Narrow crossing over a deep chasm
- Venn's Cabin - Remote dwelling where the Waterkeeper stays
- Water channels - Ancient irrigation system feeding the farms below

## Notes
- Travel requires winter gear on lower slopes
- Dangerous wildlife includes wolves and territorial creatures
