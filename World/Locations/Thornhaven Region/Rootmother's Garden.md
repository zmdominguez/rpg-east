## Description

A desert sanctuary located beyond [[Cloudbreak Pass]] on the plateau above the [[Veiled Peaks]]. Accessible through a half-buried stone arch. Home to rare plants including [[Blood root|Blood Root]], which heals the land rather than people.

## Notable Figures

- [[Pipe Ashford]] — Guardian of the garden

## Notable Events

- [[S05 Finding Pipe]] — The party found Pipe alive here; [[Cross Bones Inc]] attacked at night and the Root Guardian animated to defend the garden
- [[S06 Chaos in the Garden]] — Cross Bones repelled: the party stole Nyra's teleportation device and sent the Root Guardian to intercept Jinx and Pipe at the mountain base; Nyra fled; [[Snag Ironvow]] remained. Pipe exchanged magic items for the party's acceptance of her choice to stay. The party performed a blood root attunement ritual with unknown long-term effects.
